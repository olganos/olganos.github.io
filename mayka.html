Hello! I am Mayka. I am a real cat - I am independent and careful. I don't trust strangers and avoid them, and I don't like when people take me. However, I love my owner! I love when she pets me and love sleep with her, but I only allow to pet me when I am ready.

Two other cats live with us and sometimes we play, but I don't like them and I prefer to watch for them. I suppose that I am a main cat in the family and sometimes I am jealous and show them who is a leader.

Fortunately, my owner know who is the main cat and respects me. I like play with the human and hang up with her. I usually lie near her or sit and watch what she does. I like when she cook something in the kitchen, even though she often cook weird dishes as salad or stew vegetables.
